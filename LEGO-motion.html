LEGO Promotional Video

I was tasked with creating a rolling promotional video to be shown on all large displays throughout LEGOLAND Windsor. I worked closely with the Licensing team and keep to the strict brand guidelines provided by LEGO.

Two variants were created, a 30 second page turner showing off the sticker book, and a 10 second quick advert. It is currently on all the screens in the gift shops and queueing areas. This was created and built in under 6 hours.
