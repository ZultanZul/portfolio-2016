Sea Hero Quest VR

I was tasked with creating an experimental Three.js banner to mimic the VR capabilities of the new Sea Hero Quest VR Game.

The Rich mobile interstitial was built using a WebGL canvas, with a stereographic video applied as a texture to the inside of a sphere. The User controlled the camera position by moving their phones. This was deployed in Gameloft games through the summer and autumn of 2017.

Projects included work in:
Storyboard, HTML5 banners, WebGL & Three.js
